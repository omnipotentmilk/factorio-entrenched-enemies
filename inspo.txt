1. Rampant
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/RampantFixed \ https://mods.factorio.com/mod/Rampant
    - New enemy types (Acid, electro, fire, ETC)
    - Drastically changed AI and aggression

2. Intelligent Enemey
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/intelligent-enemy
    - No fundamental changes, only AI changes

3. Dinural Dynamics
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/diurnal-dynamics?from=search
    - Configurable Day/Night
    - Configurable Darkness
    - Bites attacking at nightfall

3. Nightfall 
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/Nightfall
    - Biters in more polluted chunks attack more at night 
    - Highly performant 

4. Rampant Evolution
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/RampantEvolution
    - Adds simple modifiers to pollution based on biter kills

5. Bugs Rule Everything Around Me
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/BREAM
    - At night, bugs will spawn in unoccupied polluted chunks, a few tiles away from structures and away from all Lights

6. Endless Enemies
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/EndlessEnemies
    - Every 20 seconds, spawn enemies on surface, and at night, spawn rate based on pollution
    - Highly performant

7. Bob's Enemies
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/bobenemies?from=search
    - Adds more tiers of biters, and more types, up to a larger vanilla types
    - Adds useless artifacts

8. Natural Evolution Enemies
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/Natural_Evolution_Enemies
    - Adds extremely variable sizes (From mini to gigantic)
    - Adds unique enemy types (Breeder, wall breacher, etc)

9. Behemoth Enemies
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/behemoth-enemies
    - Adds gigantic version of extraplanetary creatures

10. Armored Biters
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/ArmouredBiters
    - Adds snappers, custom modeled biters with high armor resistance

11. Swarmageddon
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/Swarmageddon
    - Each biter spawns another with 50% hp, with other mods spawns the previous tier of biters

12. N in one 
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/N_biters_in_1
    - Same as Swarmageddon, but only spawns the previous tier and works in 2.0

13. Combat Mechanics Overhaul
    - https://mods.factorio.com/mod/combat-mechanics-overhaul
    - Walls block enemy projectiles
